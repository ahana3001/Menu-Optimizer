Selection & Reproduction

Elitism preserves the top 5 chromosomes each generation. New offspring form via random-point crossover from the top 15 parents. Mutation randomly swaps two genes to maintain diversity and escape local optima.

Chromosome Encoding

Each chromosome is a length-7 permutation of dish indices — one unique dish per day. Duplicate dishes within a week are not permitted, ensuring full menu variety.

Calorie Scoring (Gaussian)

Nutritional fitness uses exp(−(avg_cal − target)² / 10000), a bell curve centered on your target. Menus far from the target receive exponentially lower scores.
